# USBDeview (NirSoft)

[USBDeview](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/usbdeview.zip) is a tool from NirSoft that scans your computer and shows you a complete list of all the USB devices that have been connected in the past. It is a fundamental tool as it gives you a detailed history of every connected peripheral.

**The most important information to look for in** [**USBDeview**](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/usbdeview.zip) **is:**

* **Device Name**: The name that the device has.
* **Last Date and Time of Connection/Disconnection**: Provides you with an accurate timeline of when the device was used.
* **Serial Number**: A unique identifier for each device.
