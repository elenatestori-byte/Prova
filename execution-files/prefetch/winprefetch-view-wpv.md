# WinPrefetch View(WPV)

[WinPrefetchView ](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/win_prefetch_view.html)is a free tool specifically designed to analyze files and present the extracted data in an easily readable and sortable table.`.pf`

Use cases for a Screenshare:

* **Execution Confirmation**: [WPV](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/win_prefetch_view.html) allows you to check if an executable file has been started and, more importantly, on what date and time, based on the timestamps of the Prefetch file.
* **Analysis of Loaded Processes**: A crucial aspect of [**WPV**](https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/win_prefetch_view.html) is its ability to show what other files and directories have been loaded by a program. For example, you can examine the entry to look for the path of a cheat client or check an entry to find the path of an injected one.`javaw.exe.jarrundll32.exe.dll`
