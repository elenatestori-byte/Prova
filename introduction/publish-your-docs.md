# Basic instrumentation

To conduct a screenshare, it is essential to have the right equipment. The basic tools serve two main purposes: to securely connect to the user's computer and to record the entire screenshare for maximum transparency and protection.

### Connection Tools <a href="#strumenti-per-la-connessione" id="strumenti-per-la-connessione"></a>

The first thing you need is software that allows you to access the user's desktop remotely. The most common and recommended are:

* [AnyDesk](https://anydesk.com/en/downloads/windows): A lightweight and fast application, widely used for its simplicity and stability in connection.
* [TeamViewer](https://www.teamviewer.com/it/download/windows/): Another popular option, known for its reliability and numerous remote management features.

### Registration and Enforcement Tools <a href="#strumenti-per-la-registrazione-e-la-tutela" id="strumenti-per-la-registrazione-e-la-tutela"></a>

Logging the audit is a critical security measure for yourself. In the event of disputes, having a complete record of the proceedings gives you irrefutable proof of what happened.

* [OBS](https://obsproject.com/download) (Open Broadcaster Software): It is a free and open-source software that is ideal for recording your screen in high quality. Its use is strongly recommended to document every step of the check.

### Privacy Tools <a href="#strumenti-per-la-privacy" id="strumenti-per-la-privacy"></a>

Using a VPN (Virtual Private Network) is an extra precaution that protects both you and the user.

* [VPN](https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rete_privata_virtuale): A service that masks your IP address and encrypts your connection. Services like [ProtonVPN ](https://protonvpn.com/it?srsltid=AfmBOopApHikFLbNdOe6HcoDu-q-4ERRk-tn2KqwGEVj1s-mk3RGsCwD)or other free and reliable VPNs are a great choice to ensure your privacy and professionalism while auditing.
