# Rules to follow

## Rules to follow

You should always stand out for integrity, professionalism, and attention to privacy. It's important that you have to follow thse rules or it might be result in a strike&#x20;

### Rules for the Screensharer <a href="#regole-per-lo-screensharer" id="regole-per-lo-screensharer"></a>

* **Need for Proof:** Never start a screenshare without a valid reason, such as a credible report or concrete evidence of suspicious in-game activity. Avoid targeting a baseless player.
* **Privacy Compliance**: Access is limited to files and folders relevant to the investigation only. Don't browse personal documents, photos, private chats, or other files unrelated to the game.
* **No Access to Sensitive Data**: Access to personal and sensitive data, including browser history, saved passwords, banking information, or any other information that is not directly related to the investigation, is strictly prohibited.
* **Recording & Transparency**: Record the entire screenshare with software like [OBS](https://obsproject.com/download). This protects you and the user from false accusations and ensures transparency of the process.
* **Do Not Manipulate**: Do not alter, modify, or delete any files on the user's computer. Your role is to observe and gather evidence, not to intervene in the system.
* **Post Screenshare Data Management**: Before publishing a screenshare video, you must compulsorily censor all personal and sensitive data. This includes:
  * **Session codes** (AnyDesk, TeamViewer)
  * **IP addresses and geolocation information**
  * **User voices** or **chats** (unless explicit permission has been given)
  * **File names** or **content** that reveal **private** information
